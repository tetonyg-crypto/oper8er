Engine System

Not one AI model.
Five specialized engines.

Oper8er analyzes every inbound message and activates the correct execution engine based on detected context. Each engine is a specialized protocol — not a general-purpose AI reply generator.

ENGINE 01
Appointment Engine
Activated by: Scheduling signals
Converts vague scheduling interest into confirmed commitments. Detects "maybe this weekend" and applies binary scheduling prompts that produce actual appointments, not open-ended maybes.
Signal Detected
"I'll try to come by sometime this week" → Engine converts to: "Saturday at 10 or Sunday at 1 — which works better?"
ENGINE 02
Objection Engine
Activated by: Friction signals
Detects price objections, hesitation, and stalling language. Surfaces structured responses that redirect to appointment scheduling rather than entering price negotiation. No discounts volunteered.
Signal Detected
"Price is too high" → Redirect to appointment with value reframe — not a discount offer.
ENGINE 03
Follow-Up Engine
Activated by: Lead silence
Generates and queues escalating follow-up sequences when leads go silent. 4-tier cadence: Day 2 Tier 1, Day 3 Tier 2, Day 6 Tier 3, Day 8 scarcity. Zero manual scheduling required.
Escalation Path
No response in 24hr → Tier 1 queued. Still silent → Tier 2. Day 8: "Had 2 other inquiries on this vehicle this week."
ENGINE 04
Qualification Engine
Activated by: Insufficient context
Detects when buyer intent, timeline, or decision signals are unclear. Guides reps through qualification sequences that surface deal-critical information without interrogating the customer.
Signal Detected
Missing: timeline, decision-maker, budget signal → Engine surfaces targeted questions that advance the deal.
ENGINE 05
Lead Recovery Engine
Activated by: Dormant leads
Re-engages cold leads beyond the standard follow-up cadence using value restatement, new information triggers, and scarcity signals. Recovery outcomes tracked as separate telemetry events.
Signal Detected
Lead silent 7+ days, not marked lost → Recovery sequence activated with contextual re-engagement strategy.

Common Questions

Got Questions?

How is this different from my CRM's built-in AI features?
Your CRM records what happened. Oper8er enforces how it happens. CRM AI features suggest things — reps still decide whether to act. Oper8er validates every interaction before it goes out, structures every CRM note automatically, and queues every follow-up without rep input. It's the enforcement layer your CRM was never designed to be.
Do my reps need training to use it?
Minimal. Oper8er works with your existing CRM — reps don't change their tools. The system surfaces guidance in the workflow they're already in. Implementation takes approximately 2 weeks, followed by a 2-week calibration period. By week four, execution discipline is running automatically.
What CRM systems does Oper8er integrate with?
Oper8er integrates via API with VinSolutions, DealerSocket, Reynolds & Reynolds, and other major automotive CRM systems. For other verticals: Wealthbox, Salesforce, HubSpot, and Follow Up Boss integrations are in development. If your CRM has an API, we can build to it. The audit process confirms compatibility before any commitment is made.
Is the AI talking to my customers directly?
No. Oper8er is not a chatbot and is not customer-facing. The system guides your reps — it surfaces structured response recommendations, and the rep reviews and sends. You can configure an approval workflow where all outbound messages require rep confirmation before sending. Your team maintains full control of customer relationships.
What happens if a lead wants to talk to a human?
Oper8er routes escalation signals immediately. When a customer requests to speak with someone or shows signals of frustration, the system flags the conversation for immediate human takeover and surfaces it in the rep's queue as a priority. The response guidance for escalation scenarios is specifically designed to facilitate — not delay — human handoff.
What does the 30-Day Execution Audit cost?

We connect to your CRM, observe 30 days of real execution, and deliver a forensic report showing your actual response times, follow-up compliance rates, CRM structure quality, and estimated revenue leakage.
